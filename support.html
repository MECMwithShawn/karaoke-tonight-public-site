Karaoke Tonight Support

Effective date: July 2, 2026

Karaoke Tonight helps you find nearby karaoke venues, view venue details, save favorites, keep local song and performance notes, plan nights with friends, and use public crowd-sourced venue information.

Get Help

For support, bug reports, or feedback, use the Karaoke Tonight support issue tracker:

https://github.com/MECMwithShawn/Karaoke-Tonight/issues

Do not post passwords, API keys, private account details, payment information, exact home addresses, or other sensitive personal information in public support requests.

What To Include

When reporting an issue, include:

  • The device model and iOS version.
  • The app build or TestFlight build number if available.
  • The city or general area where the issue happened.
  • Whether the Results source showed Google Places or Demo/Mock data.
  • A short description of what you tapped and what happened.
  • Screenshots only if they do not expose sensitive information.

Privacy And Data

Karaoke Tonight does not create or require an app account in this release lane.

Profile, songbook, performance history, and Friends social data are local-only in this lane. Uninstalling the app or clearing app storage can remove local-only data.

The current lane does not request Contacts permission, upload profile photos, pick photos from your phone, send automatic friend notifications, or include advertising SDKs. No real purchases, subscriptions, StoreKit transactions, or RevenueCat entitlements are active in this lane.

See the Karaoke Tonight Privacy Policy for more detail before submitting support requests with app behavior or data questions.

Current Release Limits

Venue search depends on location permission and Google Places when live credentials are configured.

Karaoke schedules and venue intelligence may include public crowd-sourced information and recent review-derived mentions. Confirm karaoke times with the venue before traveling.

Paid preview is a local preview in the current lane. Real purchases and subscriptions are not active.
