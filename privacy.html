Karaoke Tonight Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Karaoke Tonight helps users find nearby karaoke venues, view venue details, save favorites, keep local song and performance notes, plan nights with local friends, and submit public crowd-sourced karaoke venue information.

Information The App Uses

Location

Karaoke Tonight asks for approximate or current location permission to search for nearby karaoke venues. Location is used for app functionality and is sent to Google Places when live venue search is configured.

Local Device Data

The app may store the following on the device using local storage:

  • Favorite venues.
  • Profile preview preferences.
  • Optional profile photo URL.
  • Songbook entries.
  • Performance history.
  • Practice vocal range.
  • Custom schedule cache.
  • Venue intel cache.
  • Local friends, Tonight Plan, invite status, and friend performance ratings.
  • Spotify OAuth tokens if Spotify connection is enabled.
  • Paid preview Preview state.

Profile, songbook, performance history, and Friends social data are local-only in the current release lane. Uninstalling the app or clearing app storage can remove this local data.

If you add a profile photo by entering an image URL, the app loads that image from the image host you choose. That host may receive the image request from your device. The current release lane does not upload profile photos, pick photos from your phone, or request photo-library access.

Accounts

Karaoke Tonight does not create or require an app account in this release.

No in-app account deletion flow is present because no Karaoke Tonight account exists in this release. Spotify sign-in or connection, if configured, is an external Spotify account connection and does not create a Karaoke Tonight account.

The Friends feature stores friend names or handles, Tonight Plan text, invite status, and friend performance ratings locally on the device. Invites use the native share sheet only. The app does not request Contacts permission, upload a friend list, sync friend activity to a backend, or send automatic notifications in this lane.

Google Places

When configured, Karaoke Tonight uses Google Places to search for venues and show venue details such as name, address, phone, website, rating, hours, open status, photos, and location.

Supabase Public Crowd Data

Karaoke Tonight may use Supabase with a public anon client for shared, crowd-sourced venue data:

  • Public karaoke schedule submissions.
  • Public venue intelligence reports such as queue length, estimated wait, crowd size, and host quality.

Personal profile, songbook, and performance history cloud sync is disabled until real account authentication and production row-level security are implemented.

Music Services

Apple Music and Spotify actions open external services. Spotify account connection, when configured, uses OAuth PKCE with a public client ID. No Spotify client secret is stored in the app.

Purchases

The current Expo Go lane uses a local Paid preview Preview only. No real purchases, subscriptions, StoreKit transactions, or RevenueCat entitlements are active in this lane.

Tracking And Advertising

Karaoke Tonight does not intentionally include advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking in the current app code.

Data Sharing

Karaoke Tonight uses third-party services only to provide app functionality:

  • Google Places for venue search and venue details.
  • Supabase for public crowd-sourced venue schedules and venue intel when configured.
  • Spotify and Apple Music when users choose to open or connect those services.
